DIGITAL 1 PORTFOLIO FALL 2020

Color Harmony

This artwork was created with a split complementary color scheme and was created from the perspective of someone looking at their own hands as their vision becomes slowly corrupted by vibrant colors.

Atmospheric Perspective

In this artwork, an other-wordly planet full of clones if portrayed. The focus of this work is to create atmospheric perspective through ways such as desaturating backgrounds and bluring lines.

Self Portrait

In this self portrait, I am pictured in a very dark room with a huge black mass emerging from my head. This represents my anxiety and the overwhelming thoughts that stem from my mind.

Logo

This logo was created for a future brand for online marketing, space_neg. The triadic color scheme was chosen because it is visually appealing and very bright in color, which catches the eye of the viewer.

Poster 

In this poster, the same color scheme was used as the logo, but with more muted colors to make it more legible. This advertisement is for a future company that makes posters, prints, clothing, and other apparel.
